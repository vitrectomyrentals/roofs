Who we are

We Don't Do Roofing.
We Do Connections.

GTA Roofing Connect is a referral service built to take the guesswork out of finding a reliable roofer in Toronto. We match homeowners with vetted local contractors — nothing more, nothing less.

Our role
The Middleman You Actually Want
Most people dread calling a contractor they don't know. That's the problem we solve.
01
We are a referral service
We connect Toronto homeowners with independent, licensed roofing contractors. We don't perform any roofing work ourselves — that's intentional.
02
We vet our contractors
Every contractor in our network carries liability insurance and WSIB coverage. We don't refer anyone we wouldn't send to our own home.
03
You deal directly with them
Once we make the match, the contractor contacts you, quotes you, and handles the work. Payment goes directly to them — not through us.
How it works

Simple by Design

We built GTA Roofing Connect to be lean and transparent. There's no hidden markup on contractor quotes, no upselling, and no pressure. You get connected, you get quoted, you decide.

Our contractors compete on quality and responsiveness — which means you benefit.

  • You submit your roofing need through our site
  • We match you with the right local contractor
  • The contractor calls you within 24 hours
  • They visit your home and provide a free written quote
  • You decide whether to proceed — no obligation
