About the service
No. GTA Roofing Connect is a roofing referral and lead generation service. We connect homeowners with independent, licensed roofing contractors in the GTA. We do not perform any roofing work ourselves.
We earn a referral fee from the contractors in our network when a successful connection is made. This fee is paid by the contractor — it is never added to your quote or charged to you in any way.
We cover the full Greater Toronto Area including Toronto, Scarborough, North York, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Pickering, Ajax, and surrounding areas. See our Areas Served page for full details.
In most cases, a local roofing specialist will contact you within 24 hours of submitting your request. For emergency jobs such as active leaks, we prioritize those connections and response times are typically faster.
The contractors
Yes. All contractors in our network are required to carry general liability insurance and WSIB (Workplace Safety and Insurance Board) coverage before we refer them to homeowners. We do not work with uninsured contractors.
